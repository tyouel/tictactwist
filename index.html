🎲 How to Play

Classic Mode

Good ol' tic-tac-toe — three in a row wins. Simple. Timeless. But don't get too comfortable. On Expert difficulty, even Classic has a twist — check out Vanishing Pieces below!

⚡ TicTacTwist Mode

This is where things get really fun. Before you place your mark, you can twist the whole board!

  • ↻ ↺ Rotate the board 45° left or right
  • ↑ ↓ ← → Slide the entire board in any direction
  • ✕ ○ Then place your mark

🧠 Transforms are available on moves 2–4 only — use them wisely!

🎯 AI Difficulty

  • Easy — the AI plays smart but never rotates or slides the board
  • Medium — the AI can use one transform per turn: a single 45° rotation or a single slide
  • Expert — the AI chains multiple rotations, slides, and combos to outsmart you

👻 Vanishing Pieces (Expert only)

Each player can only have 3 pieces on the board at a time. When you place a 4th, your oldest piece vanishes!

  • Your oldest piece blinks to warn you it's next to go
  • Plan ahead — the board is always shifting beneath you
  • There's no "safe" lead when pieces keep disappearing!

💡 Pro Tips

  • Hit H anytime for a Hint — the AI will show you its best move
  • Stack multiple rotations and shifts before placing (moves 2–4)
  • Watch out — your opponent can twist the board too!
