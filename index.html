🎲 How to Play

Classic Mode

Good ol' tic-tac-toe — three in a row wins. Simple. Timeless. You know the drill.

⚡ TicTacTwist Mode

This is where things get wild. Before you place your mark, you can twist the whole board!

  • ↻ ↺ Rotate the board 45° left or right
  • ↑ ↓ ← → Slide the entire board in any direction
  • ✕ ○ Then place your mark

🧠 Transforms are available on moves 2–5 only — use them wisely!

🎯 AI Difficulty

  • Easy — the AI plays smart but never rotates or slides the board
  • Medium — the AI can use a single rotation (45°) or a single slide per turn
  • Expert — the AI uses multiple rotations, slides, and combos to outsmart you

💡 Pro Tips

  • Hit H anytime for a Hint — the AI will show you its best move
  • Stack multiple rotations and shifts before placing
  • Watch out — your opponent can twist the board too!
