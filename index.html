Welcome to TicTacTwist!

How to play: Place your mark (X or O) to make 3 in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The Twist: The board can slide and rotate! Rotate the board or slide rows/columns to change the game state — strategy + chaos!

Controls: Tap a cell to place a mark. Use the rotate and slide controls (or gestures) to twist the board.

Tip: Think two moves ahead — your twist can turn your loss into a win!

Quick Moves & Rotation

  • Move: Drag a row/column handle or use the onscreen arrows to slide.
  • Rotate: Use the rotate buttons or two-finger rotate gesture on touch devices.
